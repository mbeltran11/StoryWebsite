College Information

by Manuel Beltran

Info on Arrupe: Loyola

Requirements:

-Enter college directly after high school.

-Possess a cumulative GPA of at least 2.5.

-Demonstrate financial need.

But Nursing students should have a GPA of at least 3.0

Courses needed to have completed during highschool, for best chance of admission:

-English courses: 4 years

-Mathematics courses: 3 years

-Science courses: 3 years

-Social science courses: 3 years

-Language courses: 2 years
